Handicraft

  • Description

    The CheeseBurgler has stolen all of Inspector Gadgets gadgets to sell them on eBayso that he has enough money to buy the Travis Scott Burger. Inspector Gadget needs the scouts help to find his gadgets and catch the CheeseBurgler.

  • Free Time Event

    Gadget scavenger hunt. A scavenger hunt to find the extend arm, rocket boots, and propellor hat, which are hidden somewhere around the area and the activity field. Scouts will follow clues/riddles to find the items and return them to inspector gadget.

    Extravaganza Event

    Gadget design contest. A contest to see who can design the best gadget to catch the Cheeseburgler. Whoever makes the most creative and effective blueprint wins. There will also be open area, (four square, gaga ball etc.)
