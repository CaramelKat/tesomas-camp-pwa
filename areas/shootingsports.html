Shooting Sports

  • Description

    Bubbles has left the trio. So Blossom, Buttercup and Professor Utonium have to find a replacement. Come to shooting sports and compete in our rifle and archery shooting competitions to earn the right to replace bubbles.

  • Free Time Event

    Shooting Competition. Highest score wins the competition. Both score 0-50 with 50 being the max. You get two chances with your highest score being your score (lower score is the tie breaker). Participating in the shooting competition will grant your troop points towards the super cup.
